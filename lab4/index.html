How to Make Strawberry Rhubarb Pie

  1. Print the recipie
  2. Gather ingredients
    3. Making Dough
  3. Mix flour, sugar butter and salt
  4. Whisk, egg, vinegar, and water in a 2-cup measuring cup
  5. pour over dry ingredients
  6. Mix together without overworking
  7. Toss additional flour over the ball of dough
  8. Chill dough
  9. Divide Dough into two disks
  10. Roll out one piece of dough to make bottom crust
  11. Place into a pie dish
  12. Put dish in fridge to Chill
  13. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  14. Mix rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, tapioca,flour, zest, lemon juice, dash of cinnamon, and vanilla
  15. Mix well in large bowl and pour out into the crust
  16. Dot the top of the filling with butter
  17. Brush edges of crust with egg white wash
  18. Roll out second piece of dough and place over Filling
  19. Crimp to seal edges and brush with egg white wash, garnish with sugar
  20. Collar with foil and bake at 425 for 15 minutes
  21. Decrease temperature to 375 for 45 to 50 minutes or until the filling starts bubbling
  22. Let cool before serving
